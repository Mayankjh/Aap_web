Aam Aadmi Party – Human Resource Management Curriculum​-

This curriculum aims towards evolving a sense of how an individual can develop the essential skills in order to manage, develop and effectively utilise the available human resource in an organisation. This is will be done through a 4-week fellowship program which will include multiple sessions revisiting the history and fundamentals of Human Resource Management and which would ultimately penetrate the concurrent issues faced by an organization regarding its human resource. Apart from the knowledge sessions, there will be multiple interactive sessions and activities which will give all the participants a chance to apply the theoretical knowledge they gain into a practical sphere. Along with this, the participants will be required to identify one contemporary HR Management issue (gender diversity, non-monetary incentivisation, cultural diversity, age diversity, etc.) and prepare a case study/dissertation on it.
