IGNITING YOUNG MINDS

Change happens when young minds, full of zest and dedication come under one umbrella, an equal plain to be the change they want to see. Aam Aadmi Fellowship brings to you an opportunity to be THE CHANGE you want to be. This opportunity is a new way of looking at things with a view, where young souls are responsible for bringing about the change in the society, for one and all. A delightful and an enriching experience for all the young fresh minds, looking for an opening to bring about a change in the society for its betterment and growth. Aam Aadmi Party strives to be that one sole platform which gives due importance and consideration to the youth for bringing about a revelation in the system, which not only affects them as being a political party but also helps them make a change and not be a mere political party in the system.
