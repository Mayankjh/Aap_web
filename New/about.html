Who we are to Serave the nation



The Aam Aadmi Party was born out of the India Against Corruption movement in 2012, and was a watershed movement in India’s political history. Since then the party has expanded its support base exponentially and continues to be the voice of the common man. As a party born out of a historic anti-corruption movement, the Aam Aadmi Party holds itself to the highest standards of probity. The party is committed to eradicating corruption from politics and continues to work towards increasing the levels of accountability, transparency and responsiveness in its governance models and decision making bodies.

At the Aam Aadmi Party, we believe all of us live within political systems and are affected by the changes in the global political economy. The youth harbours distinctive political interests, is free of associations of excesses of the past, more idealistic in their goals and less loyal to established traditions. Through this fellowship programme, we hope to deepen one’s knowledge and understanding of one of the most powerful forces operating on people, communities and corporations today, namely government and politics in India. And being a part of this phenomenon, dealing with systems of governance, and the analysis of political activities, political thoughts and political behaviour, would provide youngsters with an excellent experience of knowledge sharing, enhancement and capacity building.

We started this internship and fellowship programme in the June of 2017, with the aim of exposing the youth of this country to the functioning of a political party. The programme would provide them with an opportunity to make their contribution in the process of nation building and learn various skills in the diverse departments. We aim for the holistic development of the interns, equipping them with skills suited for a civil, political and social life; provide them with insights into the political, governmental and social institutions and help the understand where India stands.

We aim to build an army of professionals who have gone through rigorous training in research, social media, public policy, legal studies, human resource management, ground level and call campaigning, for the purpose of constant efficient, effective and structured functioning in various sectors of our party.

We wish to help empower young minds become the agents of change through leadership and meaningful participation in political and social life, enabling them to realize their full potential and contribute to their community.
