Legal Research

About

The legal research course will teach you how to think and write like a lawyer and will include learning the most common legal research techniques such as interpretation of statues and finding case laws. The curriculum will dwell into the functioning of the GNCTD and the legal issues they face on various administrative and legal fronts and their possible solutions and topics of National Legal Importance. The fellowship will also concentrate on research papers that will be made by the interns and interactive sessions amongst the interns for the purpose of broadening the understanding of the system, issues and the possible solutions.

Curriculum

The Week wise curriculum will be as follows

Week 1

  • Explaining Legal research, Sources of Legal research and utility of Legal research.
  • Research on Infringement of Privacy through Aadhar.

Week 2

  • Introduction to Multiplicity of authority in Delhi and how law and order is not under the control of Delhi Government and its implication on the functioning of the state and on the citizens.
  • Comparison of the situation and with other states and other Union Territories and other country capitals.
  • Possible solutions to the Problems of Delhi.

Week 3

  • Introduction to RTI, its history and its importance.
  • Research and analysis on the recent amendments in the RTI act and its legal and political implications.
  • Research and analysis of witness protection laws in India. With various case laws.
