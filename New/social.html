About the Social Media and IT Cell:

In this era of technology- blogs, digital archives, e-readers, and social media sites like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram have revolutionized the concept of political discourse by changing how we share and transmit ideas and information. We, at the Aam Aadmi Party believe that digital media or social media can be used effectively to galvanize the energy around particular political issues.Platforms like Twitter and Facebook, give political organisations the ability to broadcast information on a worldwide stream, join any ongoing debates and discussions and have a two-way interaction with the public during political processes and campaigns. Social media is the only way to connect and reach out to the youth, involve them in the decision making process thereby helping them make informed choices and help strengthen the roots of democracy.

The IT Cell at Aam Aadmi Party continuously works towards increasing the visibility and expanding its reach with the help of online tools that are automation based. Knowing that traditional means like door-to door campaigning and rallies have limited reach towards millennials, we make sure that the right messaging, which includes area specific content, graphic and videos made exclusively for targeted audiences, are sent out through such platforms. We encourage usage of technology in the public policies domain and social media marketing.

About the Social Media and IT Cell:

Graphic Designing-

It is the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas and experiences with visual and textual content. It helps create content that can shape and influence one’s understanding of modern protest, activism, politics and power too. It involves a creative and systematic plan to achieve certain objectives, with the use of images, symbols or even words. It is visual communication and the aesthetic expression of concepts and ideas using various graphic elements and tools.

Content Writing–

The Department aims at producing engaging content for both online and offline audiences. It includes generating tightly focussed content for blogs, articles, tweets, videos, social media posts, etc. that are written with great clarity and are full of persuasive power.

Videography/Photography –

Teams that work on producing video films for various social media platforms keeping in mind the target audiences and generating simple, yet hard hitting content that engages with the audiences.

Caricature -

Caricatures are laced with subtle messages and satire, and wield significant power with their pen. They transmit messages without the need for the written word and are consumed by people across all the sections of society.

Social Media Marketing –

It refers to the process of gaining traffic or attention through social media sites and pushing one’s content and agenda. Establishing a communication network that helps syndicate content and increase online visibility, all while engaging with a broad audience of consumers.

IT-

Creating Websites and developing apps and software and to execute digital transformation at scale, properly equipped with the right skills and expertise and a shared purpose that drives the work.

Curriculum

The Week wise curriculum will be as follows

Week 1

  • Social Media And IT Cell Induction
  • Developing political understanding
  • Working on different social media platforms
  • Usage and generation of content for social media and IT
  • Brief about the roles and responsibilities of different departments.

Week 2

  • Training Week
  • Graphic and Caricature design team –Learn to use Adobe Photoshop, CorelDraw, Adobe Indesign, Adobe Illustrator, etc., editing software.
  • Content Writing team –Writing exercises to develop critical thinking skills and enhance political understanding. Working on vocabulary and writing structures and different writing formats.
  • Videography/Photography team- Gadget handling, manual mode, field work, understanding the subject and a number of videography techniques, etc.
  • IT team – Skill development, learning different programming and scripting languages, brief about CMS and learning about the purpose of IT tools development.

Week 3

  • Working on News Reading and real time work.
  • IT Prototype generationtions.
  • Guest Sessions
  • Field work for Videography and Photography team
  • News Tracking
  • Projects and Documentaries
  • Learning about real-time, long-term and campaigning methods

Week 4

  • Field tours
  • Guest sessions
  • Learning about the strategies of campaigning
  • A Day with a Political Leader
  • Working for Social Media Platforms and learning social media analytics and scheduling techniques.
